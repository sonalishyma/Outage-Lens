Duration Model
The model that predicts how long an outage will last, given its cause, region, season and time of day.
What this model does
Given cause category, climate region, NERC region, season, month, year, hour the outage started, and local population density, it predicts outage duration — reframed as log1p(minutes) and fit with gradient boosting, since raw-minute regression is dominated by a long tail of multi-week outages.
